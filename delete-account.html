Delete Your RezSpot Account

You can request deletion of your RezSpot account and all associated data by following the steps below or by contacting us directly.

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be removed within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Your profile information (name, email, avatar)
  • All resources you have created
  • All booking history
  • All availability schedules and services
  • Your subscription (cancellation will be processed automatically)

Option 1 — Delete from within the app

  1. Open RezSpot and sign in
  2. Tap the Settings tab
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

Send a deletion request to privacy@rezspot.com from the email address associated with your account. Include "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line. We will process your request within 30 days.

Questions

If you have any questions about account deletion or your data, contact us at privacy@rezspot.com.
